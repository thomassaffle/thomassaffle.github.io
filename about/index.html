About

Thomas Saffle is product designer and illustrator based in Seattle, WA.

Thomas has five years of experience working as a freelance product designer and illustrator. He strives to bring quality designs under deadlines to his clients.

Thomas was born and raised in Virginia but also lived in a number of European countries throughout his childhood. Immersion in these various cultures embedded a love of visual art and design from an early age on. He received his BFA in studio art from the University of Pennsylvania in conjunction with The Pennsylvania Academy of Fine Arts in Philadelphia. Thomas earned his MFA in Visual Communications from the University of Arizona in 2015.

Earlier this year Thomas moved with his wife to Seattle and they are currently expecting their first child.
